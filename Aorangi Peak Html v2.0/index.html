HISTORY & LEGEND

It was established in 1937, originated from earlier ice-cream and candy stores and now as the most renowned restaurants in New Zealand. With the beautiful and romantic legend background, it was the place of "Where the Fairy People Lived".

LOCATION

Aorangi Peak Complex is situated over 2,000 feet above sea level and it is the highest point you may drive up to in the district.Only 15 minutes scenic drive from Rotorua city.

RELAX & DINING

Aorangi Peak retreat is the perfect place to relax, unwind and escape from the stress of your busy life. Indulge yourself with a cup of organic coffee made of 300 year-old underground water and award winning cuisine.

COMPLEX FUNCTIONS

Under his complex include wedding & function centre, holiday villas, deer and alpaca farm.. It has also been the most popular location for photographers and movies scene for International TV series and movies.

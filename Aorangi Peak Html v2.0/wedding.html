  • Welcome

    Aorangi Peak Restaurant would like to congratulate you on your engagement.Planning your up coming wedding is a special and exciting time.

    We welcome this opportunity to invite you to view some of Rotorua's most best kept secrets. We have the most breathtaking panoramic views, Stunning lush native surroundings And exquisite fine dinning,

    What ever it maybe big or small our experienced staff are only more than happy to cater to your desires insuring that your special day is a unforgettable and memorable one shared with family and friends.

  • Ihenga VIP Room


    Aorangi Peak has a VIP Room available for hire to individuals or small groups.We have hosted many small wedding parties in this room and it is an outstanding, first class venue. This room is available for hire for up to 20 people. If you are interested in hiring this VIP Room for your function, please feel free to discuss the terms with the Aorangi Peak Wedding and Group Coordinator.

  • Mokoia Room


    The Mokoia Room is our a la carte, fine dining room and can be booked for wedding parties of up to 70 people. This room is a delightful venue for a wedding, and is ideally suited to smaller groups with it's rounded shape and split level, flowing layout.

  • Tarawera Room


    A large room with magnificent views, this room is ideally suited to larger weddings of between 70 and 200 people. It has easy access to the outside area, has a covered outdoor veranda area and a large stage for entertainment etc.
